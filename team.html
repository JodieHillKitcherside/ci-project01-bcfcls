Rules

Apperance points are regardless of minutes played.

Match points 'goal' and 'assist' will be determined on the postion of the player at the time of each event. 'Clean Sheet' will be awarded on the postion of the player spent most during the game.

For example, if a player spent more time in DEF, they may be awarded a clean sheet.

Gaffer's MOM can be split if no clear winner or more than one player considered.

For example, 3pts for Player 1, 2 points for Player 2 etc.

Extras

We will have a 'Player of the month' based on your fantasy score. No prize, just bragging rights and a shout out on our socials.

The overall fantasy winner at the end of the season will get a 'fantasy' trophy and free signing fee for the next season.

Winner to be announced at the End of Season Drinks.
